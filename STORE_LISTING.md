## Name
Highcloak — AI Data Protection

## Short Description (132 char max)
Detects SSNs, credit cards, and personal data in AI prompts before they're sent. Runs locally. We never see your data.

## Detailed Description

Highcloak catches personal data in your AI prompts before they leave your machine.

Paste a customer's Social Security number into ChatGPT? Highcloak blocks it. Type a credit card number into Claude? Blocked. Phone number in Gemini? You'll see a warning.

WHAT IT DETECTS
- Social Security numbers
- Credit card numbers (with Luhn validation)
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- IP addresses
- Dates of birth
- US passport numbers
- Driver's license numbers

HOW IT WORKS
1. You type into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Copilot
2. Highcloak scans your text locally in the browser
3. If PII is found, you see a warning or the send button is blocked
4. Choose to redact the data or remove it yourself

YOUR DATA NEVER LEAVES YOUR MACHINE
All detection runs locally using pattern matching in the browser. Highcloak does not send your text anywhere. No cloud processing. No server required. No account needed.

Don't trust us — verify. The detection engine is open source.

CONFIGURABLE POLICIES
- Block: SSNs, credit cards, passport numbers, driver's license numbers (send is disabled)
- Warn: Names, phone numbers, IP addresses, dates of birth (you choose what to do)
- Allow: Email addresses (logged only)

WORKS ACROSS EVERY AI TOOL
One extension covers ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot. You don't need a separate tool for each.

FREE. NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED.
Install and start detecting PII in under 60 seconds. No sign-up, no configuration, no server.

Coming soon: centralized policies, audit trails, and compliance reports for teams.
